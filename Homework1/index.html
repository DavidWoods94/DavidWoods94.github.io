Super Smash Brothers

Super Smash Brothers is a videogame series created by Nintendo.

The games are fighting/party games that feature some of Nintendo's most popular videogame characters such as Mario, Link , and Pikachu.

It's been more than 15 years since the first game was released on the Nintendo 64 and since then the series has seen an increasing number of fighters and an increasing fanbase.
